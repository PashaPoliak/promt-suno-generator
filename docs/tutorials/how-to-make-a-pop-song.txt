Creating Your AI Pop Song with Suno
Understanding Suno Prompts
A suno prompt is a textual input that guides Suno AI in generating music tailored to your specifications. Crafting an effective suno prompt is crucial for producing a suno pop song that aligns with your artistic vision. The prompt should include details about the genre, mood, tempo, instrumentation, and lyrical themes to ensure the AI understands your requirements.
Crafting the Perfect Suno Prompt
To create a hit AI pop song, your prompt Suno should be clear, specific, and rich in descriptive language. Here’s how to structure your prompt:
Genre
: Specify that you’re creating a pop song.
Mood and Atmosphere
: Describe the emotional tone (e.g., upbeat, romantic, energetic).
Tempo and Rhythm
: Indicate the desired beats per minute (BPM) and rhythmic feel.
Instrumentation
: Mention the instruments you want featured (e.g., synths, guitars, drums).
Lyrical Themes
: Outline the subject matter or story of the song.
Structure
: Define the song structure (e.g., verse-chorus-verse).
Additional Elements
: Add any other desired features (e.g., layered harmonies, polished production quality).
Example Suno Prompt :
Pop, upbeat, romantic, 120 BPM, catchy danceable beats,
energetic synths, acoustic guitar, summer love, dynamic chorus

Genre
: Clearly defines the style of the song.
Mood
: Sets the emotional tone for the music.
Tempo
: Specifies the speed, which is crucial for danceability.
Rhythm

Generating Your AI Pop Song
Follow these steps to generate your AI pop song using Suno:
Access Suno AI
: Log in to your Suno AI account. If you don’t have one, sign up on the
Suno AI website
.
Navigate to the Music Generation Tool
: Select the option to create a new song.
Enter Your Suno Prompt
: Input your crafted suno prompt into the prompt field.
Set Parameters
: Adjust any additional settings, such as song length or key signature, if available.
Generate the Song
: Click the “Generate” button and wait for Suno AI to create your song.
Review and Refine
: Listen to the generated song. If it doesn’t meet your expectations, refine your suno prompt with more specific details and regenerate.
Tip:
Experiment with different prompt variations to explore diverse musical possibilities and find the perfect fit for your vision.
Mastering your Suno song
There is a great free service to master your suno song: Bandlab. They offer a few different mastering styles for free that work for different styles outputted by Suno. I chose the universal setting for the example song, this setting works well for Pop, EDM, Rock and Alternative suno songs.
Designing Your Album Cover with Midjourney
Integrating Your AI Pop Song Concept
Your album cover is a visual representation of your AI pop song. It should capture the essence of your music and attract listeners’ attention. By using Midjourney, you can create a unique and professional album cover that complements your song’s theme and mood.
Crafting Midjourney Prompts for Album Covers
To design an impactful album cover, your Midjourney prompt should include elements that reflect your song’s characteristics. Consider the following components:
Visual Theme
: Align with the song’s mood (e.g., vibrant, romantic, energetic).
Color Scheme
: Choose colors that evoke the desired emotions.
Imagery and Symbols
: Incorporate symbols or imagery related to your song’s themes.
Style and Aesthetics
: Define the artistic style (e.g., abstract, minimalist, realistic).
Example MidJourney Prompt:
The key to getting a real album cover: use your gut and pick the one that feels right for the song. This is the human factor that is needed, you must curate the vibe you want to give off.
/imagine vibrant summer sunset with glowing neon lights, abstract shapes,
romantic silhouettes, in the style of modern pop art, bright colors, high contrast --ar 1:1
Explanation of the Prompt Structure:
Visual Theme
: “vibrant summer sunset” sets the scene and mood.
Color Scheme
: “glowing neon lights, bright colors” specifies the color palette.
Imagery and Symbols
: “abstract shapes, romantic silhouettes” adds visual elements that relate to the song’s themes.
Style and Aesthetics
: “modern pop art, high contrast” defines the artistic style.
Aspect Ratio
: “—ar 1:1” ensures the album cover is square, ideal for most streaming platforms.
